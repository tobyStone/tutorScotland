🎓 For Tutors

Join a professional network that raises the standard of tutoring across Scotland.

Whether you're a full-time tutor, teaching part-time, or just starting your tuition journey, Tutors Alliance Scotland is here to support, connect, and represent you.

Connect with Us

💡 Why Join TAS?

  • ✅ Be Recognised as a professional tutor through our membership framework
  • 🛡️ Stand Out with our badge of quality and shared code of conduct
  • 📚 Access CPD & Training to support your ongoing development
  • 💬 Join a Supportive Community of like-minded educators
  • 📣 Have Your Voice Heard on the future of tuition in Scotland
  • 💼 Gain Opportunities through our partnerships and outreach projects

🧩 What We Offer

📜 A Professional Membership Framework

Set your tutoring apart with our clear membership levels, built on experience, training and safeguarding practices.

📖 Ongoing CPD and Training Opportunities

From safeguarding and SEN training to subject-specific refreshers, we offer accessible, practical development – because tuition should be evidence-based, inclusive, and high quality.

👥 Networking and Peer Support

Join local and national meet-ups, online forums and discussion groups with other tutors across Scotland.

📢 Advocacy and Representation

We are building a national voice for tutors in Scotland. By joining TAS, you're helping shape the future of tutoring as a recognised, respected profession.

🔍 Who Can Join?

  • Qualified teachers transitioning into tutoring
  • University lecturers and academics
  • Newly qualified teachers
  • Retired education professionals
  • Tutors with specific expertise (e.g. SEND, maths, exam prep)

🛠️ Membership Levels

Aspiring Tutor (Student Teacher Member)

  • Access to foundational CPD modules
  • Listing in the TAS directory with "Student Teacher Member" badge
  • Discounted rates for TAS events and workshops
  • Access to tutor mentorship programs

Accredited Tutor

  • Full access to TAS CPD platform
  • Priority listing in the TAS directory
  • Use of TAS Accredited Tutor badge
  • Free PVG check processing
  • Access to online resources for compliance
  • Invitations to exclusive online networking events

Affiliated Tutor (Agency/Business Link)

  • All benefits of Accredited Tutor
  • Recognition of affiliation (agency or business) on TAS profile
  • Access to joint marketing opportunities with affiliated agency

Tutoring Agency/Business (Small)

Up to £250,000 turnover

  • Membership for up to 3 tutors
  • Agency profile in TAS directory
  • Access to business development resources
  • Discounted rates for tutors on CPD courses
  • Support with regulatory compliance

Tutoring Agency/Business (Large)

Over £250,000 turnover a year

  • All benefits of Small
  • Membership for up to 6 tutors
  • Priority placement in TAS directory
  • Feature in TAS promotional materials

Large Franchise/National Organisation

  • Access to TAS CPD resources for teaching staff
  • Institution profile in TAS directory
  • Opportunities for collaborative events
