Why Join the Scottish Tutors Association?

As a member of the STA, you will gain access to exclusive benefits designed to elevate your tutoring career:

? Professional Development: Access tailored CPD opportunities, expert-led webinars and in-depth training to expand your tutoring knowledge and skills.
? Certification Support: Stand out as a professional tutor with recognition that parents can know and trust.
? Exclusive Networking: Gain access to exclusive networking opportunities to connect with other professional tutors in Scotland.
? Tutor Hub: Gain access to resources and training specifically designed and created for tutors.
? Increased Visibility: Feature in our online tutor directory, helping parents and schools connect with qualified tutors in their area in Scotland.
? Advocacy & Support: Take advantage of our ongoing efforts to represent tutors within Scotland's education sector, ensuring your voice is heard and helping to shape the future of tuition in Scotland.

Find out more about membership.

Our STRIVE Values

At the Scottish Tutors Association, we believe in our core values which underpin all that we do.

Support – Equipping tutors with resources, guidance, and mentorship.
Training – Providing access to CPD opportunities and tailored development.
Recognition – Promoting tutor excellence through accreditation and standards.
Inclusion – Creating an inclusive network that welcomes all tutors.
Visibility – Increasing tutor presence in communities and online.
Excellence – Encouraging high standards in tutoring practice to ensure impactful learning.
