About us

Raising Standards and Shaping Futures Through Tuition

Tutors Alliance Scotland is a not-for-profit professional membership organisation created by experienced educators for fully qualified teachers who tutor in Scotland. We are committed to setting high standards and to raise the profile of tutoring across Scotland.

We are here to set a standard, support tutors, guide parents and ensure every pupil has access to safe, high-quality tuition, no matter their background or postcode.

We believe that tutoring is a powerful tool for bridging the attainment gap in Scotland. By providing tutors with resources, training and professional recognition, we ensure that families can trust and access the best possible support for their children.

👩‍🏫 For Tutors – A Professional Home for Tutors in Scotland

🎒 For Pupils – Because Every Learner Deserves to Thrive

Whether it's about catching up, getting ahead or gaining confidence, the right tuition can transform a child's learning journey.

We're working to make this a reality by:

  • 🎓 Connecting pupils with trusted, trained tutors
  • 💬 Creating safe, positive learning environments
  • 🧩 Supporting pupils with additional support needs (ASN)
  • 🎁 Offering funded tuition to pupils facing disadvantage
  • 🧭 Providing goal-led tuition that builds confidence and closes gaps
