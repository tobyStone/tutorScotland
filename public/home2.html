About Us

The Tutors Alliance Scotland (STA) is the professional membership organisation for tutors in Scotland, committed to setting high standards, supporting tutors and ensuring every child has access to high-quality tuition. The STA believe that tutoring is a powerful tool for bridging the attainment and poverty gaps in Scotland. By providing tutors with resources, training and professional recognition, we ensure that families can trust and access the best possible support for their children. Want to know more...
