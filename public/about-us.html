üí¨ About Us

Created by educators. Powered by purpose. Focused on every child.

Tutors Alliance Scotland began with a simple belief: that every child deserves access to safe, high-quality tuition, and that tutors deserve recognition, support and standards that match the difference they make.

We are a not-for-profit professional membership organisation, run by experienced educators who've spent decades in the classroom, in leadership and in the world of tutoring. We know the power of one-to-one support. We've seen how it can build confidence, close gaps and change lives especially for those who need it most.

We also know that tuition in Scotland is unregulated, inconsistent and unequal. Too many families don't know who to trust. Too many children are missing out. Too many brilliant tutors are working in isolation, without recognition or resources.

That's why we created TAS.

We're here to set a national standard for tutoring in Scotland, a standard built on trust, transparency and training. We support tutors to develop their skills. We help parents find the right support. And we work with partners and organisations to make tuition more accessible for children who need it most.

ü§ù Working in Partnership

We know the power of collaboration. That's why partnerships are central to what we do.

TAS aims to work alongside:

  • Schools
  • Local authorities
  • Third sector and youth organisations
  • Charities and community groups
  • Existing tutoring and education providers

By working together, we can:

  • Ensure tutoring reaches the children who need it most
  • Set shared standards for quality, safety and impact
  • Promote equity and access to tuition
  • Build a Scotland-wide ecosystem of trusted tutoring

üéØ Supporting Pupils Across Scotland

At the heart of Tutors Alliance Scotland is a simple goal to help children thrive.

We know that when tutoring is safe, consistent and tailored to each child, it can make a real difference to their confidence, wellbeing and long-term learning.

TAS supports pupils by:

  • Ensuring tutors are trained, vetted and committed to high standards
  • Encouraging tutors to use inclusive, evidence-based approaches
  • Championing the role of emotional wellbeing in academic progress
  • Working with partners to make tuition more accessible to those who need it most
  • Helping families make informed choices about their child's education

Our tutors don't just "teach", they connect, motivate and guide.
